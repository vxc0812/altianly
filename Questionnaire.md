## Summary

Once you submit this questionnaire, Altianly will analyze your responses and generate a personalized workout plan using AI. The plan will consider your:
- Age and fitness level
- Primary goal and timeline
- Available equipment and training days
- Any health considerations or limitations
- Training preferences

*All data stays on your device. No information is shared or stored online.*

   - Source: [ZenFit AI Quiz](https://www.zenfit-ai.fit/quiz)

4. **JEFIT** - Workout planning with goal-specific programs and equipment tracking
   - Source: [JEFIT Website](https://www.jefit.com/)

5. **Reps & Runs** - Detailed fitness assessment including body type, measurements, and workout history
   - Source: [Reps & Runs Questionnaire](https://repsandruns.com/questionnaire/)

6. **FitBudd** - Personal trainer questionnaire covering medical history, lifestyle, exercise history, and coaching style preferences
