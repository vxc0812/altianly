## Layout Posture Rules
- Border radius: 8-12px for components
- No shadows except dropdowns/modals
- Hairline borders (1px) for separation
- Tight letter-spacing on display sizes (-0.02em)
- One accent color, used at most twice per screen
- Clear hierarchy with visible hierarchy between content areas

## Fitness/Energetic Adaptation
- Primary accent (#c96442 from original) for energy and motivation
- Green-ish (success) for achievement and progress indicators
- Orange/red (danger/warn) for alerts and important actions
- Clean, energetic white background for clarity
- Dark text for readability
