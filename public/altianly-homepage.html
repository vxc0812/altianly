Features

Everything you need to stay on track

From BMI tracking to AI-generated workouts, Altianly is your all-in-one fitness companion.

⚖

BMI Calculator

Instant BMI with CDC-standard evaluation. Supports imperial and metric units. Auto-saves every entry with weight tracking and streak counter.

☢

AI Workout Plans

Generate structured plans via OpenRouter, Ollama, HuggingFace, or Cloudflare Workers AI. Warmup, sets, reps, rest, cooldown — fully personalized.

☷

Smart Questionnaire

Tell us about your lifestyle, experience, equipment, injuries, goals, and more. Every answer shapes a hyper-personalized workout plan.

☰

Progress Graphs

SVG line charts with BMI/Weight toggle and time aggregation (Days/Weeks/Months/Years). Min, max, average stats at a glance.

📋

Notion Export

Export structured workout plans directly to your Notion database. Includes name, date, BMI, warmup, exercises, cooldown, and notes.

🔒

Passkey Auth (Web)

Register and login with Windows Hello, Touch ID, or Face ID via WebAuthn. No passwords. Local fallback when offline.

🎨

Dual Theme

Dark mode (GitHub-dark inspired) and warm Cream mode. Toggle anytime — your preference is persisted across sessions.

🏆

Badges & Streaks

Earn badges for daily check-ins and workout milestones. Stay motivated with gamified progress tracking.

📝

Workout Logging

Log actual sets, reps, and weight per exercise. Day-by-day tracking tied to saved plans. Review past logs anytime.

How It Works

Three steps to your perfect workout

No account setup required. Enter your metrics, answer a few questions, and let AI do the rest.

1

Enter Your Metrics

Age, gender, height, weight. Altianly calculates your BMI and evaluates your current fitness level.

2

Answer the Questionnaire

Select your goals, experience, available equipment, training split preference, and any health considerations.

3

Get Your AI Plan

A structured workout plan with warmup, exercises, sets, reps, rest, cooldown, and notes. Save, share, or export it.
